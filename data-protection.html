Data Protection

Springwala values your trust and is dedicated to safeguarding your personal data. We implement comprehensive technical, organizational, and physical security measures to shield your data from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration.

1. Customer Data Protection

All sensitive customer details, including contact profiles, account credentials, and mailing preferences, are stored on secure servers with restricted access. We employ strong AES-256 encryption for data at rest and robust firewalls to monitor and defend our databases from unauthorized network incursions.

2. Secure Transactions

Every commercial transaction processed through Springwala.in is protected using Industry-Standard Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) or Transport Layer Security (TLS) encryption protocol. We do not store full credit card numbers or raw bank credentials on our systems; all payments are routed securely via PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways.

3. Privacy Handling

We process personal data strictly in adherence to the consent you provide during registration or order placement. You retain full control over your profile details, and you have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your stored information at any time by contacting our support desk.

4. Third-Party Sharing Policy

We do not sell, rent, or trade customer information to marketing firms or database brokers. Data sharing is strictly confined to trusted service partners (such as shipping carriers, warehouse operators, and payment processors) who require the details solely to fulfill transactions on our behalf, under binding confidentiality terms.
