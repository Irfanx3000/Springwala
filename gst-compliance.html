GST Compliance

Springwala is committed to absolute transparency and compliance with all indirect tax laws. In accordance with the Goods and Services Tax (GST) regulations enacted by the Government of India, we strictly enforce compliant tax billing and reporting practices.

1. GST Invoice Information

Every commercial transaction on Springwala.in is accompanied by a valid, itemized tax invoice. All tax invoices outline the HSN (Harmonized System of Nomenclature) code, product description, unit prices, discount rates, CGST, SGST/UTGST, or IGST breakdowns, and our corporate GST details. Business customers can input their GSTIN during checkout to receive tax-compliant invoices for input tax credit (ITC) purposes.

2. Tax Compliance Statement

We certify that Springwala, its parent entities, and associated marketplace sellers are fully registered and in active compliance with local GST filings, tax collections, and remittances. We carry out regular internal audits and work closely with tax consultants to adapt to changing tax laws, rate revisions, and state-specific tax policies.

3. Business Registration Info

To list items on our storefront, all marketplace sellers and vendors must submit verified, active GSTIN documents. Registration certificates, bank account records, and PAN info are thoroughly checked prior to account activation to prevent fraudulent tax practices and maintain high transaction integrity.

4. Applicable Tax Handling

GST percentages are calculated dynamically at checkout based on standard HSN product categories and the shipping destination. Intra-state orders attract CGST and SGST/UTGST, whereas inter-state orders attract IGST. Any return or replacement triggering an invoice adjustment will result in a corresponding Credit or Debit Note processed under GST guidelines.
