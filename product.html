Product Description

This heavy-duty die spring is engineered for applications that require high load capacity, consistent force output, and controlled deflection. Manufactured using rectangular wire, the spring is designed to distribute load evenly, resulting in improved durability and reduced fatigue under repetitive compression cycles.

The spring is suitable for use in die and mould assemblies, press tools, jigs, fixtures, and various industrial mechanisms where space constraints demand high performance in compact form. Its construction allows it to operate reliably under continuous working conditions while maintaining dimensional stability.

Designed for precision-oriented environments, this die spring provides predictable compression behaviour, making it ideal for tooling systems that require accuracy, repeatability, and long service life. The surface finish and material selection contribute to resistance against wear during prolonged operation.

This product is intended for general industrial use and is commonly selected for applications involving moderate to heavy loads, short to medium stroke lengths, and repeated compression cycles.
